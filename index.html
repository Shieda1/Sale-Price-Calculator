What is the sale price?
The sale price is a discount on a product by a specific percentage so thes means the price of this product is reduced by that percent.
How to calculate the sale price?
To calculate the sale price use the following formula:
final price = original price x percent off
Example: A product that costs $250 has a discount of 20% calculate the final price of this product?
final price = original price x percent off
final price = $250 x 0.2
final price = $50 final price of the product
Why use the sale price?
Sale price allow you to find the reduced price of a product and the amount of money you save and also to calculate the size of the discount of the original price.
